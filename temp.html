Common Tasks

Viewing Appointments

The "Appointments" menu dropdown lets you view listings of appointments.

Calendar Views

The Weekly Calendar menu item shows a weekly calendar of appointments, and Monthly Calendar shows all appointments for a given month.

Tabular Listings

The My Current and Upcoming Appointments menu item shows your appointments for today as well as upcoming appointments. The All Appointments menu item shows all appointments.

Adding Appointments

Select "New" from the "Appointments" menu

or

Use the "Customers" menu dropdown to open a customer listing. Then, after opening the customer record, and click the "Add Appointment" button at the bottom." />

Appointment Reporting

From the "Reports" menu dropdown, select Types by Month to see a bar chart showing the number of appointments for each apointment type in a given month. Use the Consultant Schedule to see appointment schedule reports for each consultant. The Appointments by Region menu option allows you to generate a pie chart of the customer nationalities for appointments in a givcen time period.

Manage Customers

Use the "Customers" menu to view a list of customers

or

Use the "Manage Addresses" menu to view a list of countries. You can then select the city and address to view customers at that specific address.

Manage Consultants

Use the "Consultants" menu to view a list of consultants.
